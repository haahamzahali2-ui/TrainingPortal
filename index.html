Athena Labelling

When a document (such as a registration form or lab result) is uploaded to Athena, it’s “Unprocessed,” meaning it is not attached to the patient and not labelled.

You will “process” the documents by following the instructions below:

  1. On the top bar of Athena, click Patients → Search Document.
  2. Set the date range to past two weeks.
  3. Select Unprocessed from the dropdown menu.
  4. Follow the remaining instructions to attach and label documents properly.
