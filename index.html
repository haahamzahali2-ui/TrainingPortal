Athena Labelling

What is Athena Labelling?

When a document (such as a registration form or lab result) is uploaded to Athena, it’s initially “Unprocessed,” meaning it’s not attached to the patient and not labelled.

You will “process” the documents by following these instructions:

  1. On the top bar of Athena, click Patients → Search Document.
  2. Set the date range to the past two weeks.
  3. Select Unprocessed from the dropdown menu.
  4. Follow the remaining internal instructions for attaching and labelling documents properly.

This demo is for review by your supervisor.
