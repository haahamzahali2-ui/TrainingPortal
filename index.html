Athena Labelling

When a document (registration form, lab result, etc.) is uploaded to Athena, it’s initially “Unprocessed” — meaning it is not attached to the patient and not labelled.

You will process the documents by following these steps:

  1. Click Patients → Search Document in Athena.
  2. Set date range to past two weeks.
  3. Select Unprocessed from dropdown.
  4. Follow remaining steps to attach and label correctly.
